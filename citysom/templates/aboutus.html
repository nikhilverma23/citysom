Our Mission

 


Want to get out? The idea to trudge through all the search engines, websites and various listings to find out what fun event is around already frustrating?

Introducing Citysom : within seconds get an overview of the best events your city has to offer, fitting your tastes, budget and agenda!

Sounds exciting? Browse through the collection of events and find what new exciting thing you're about to do!

The city is yours, let Citysom help you make the most of it!

The Team

 


The idea of an online city guide came during a discussion between friends in Boston. They were graduate students, transplanted in a new city and couldn’t find easily what to do.

One of these students is me, Charles-Henri. I am from Paris, France and find it is way too complicated to find out what is going on with the current tools at hand. So I built Citysom for all the people who want to discover new fun things but don’t want to spend the day searching for it.

I hope you will enjoy this service and that it will help you enrich your free-time!

See you out there!

Charles-Henri
